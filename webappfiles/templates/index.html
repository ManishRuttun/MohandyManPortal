Housekeeper

Housekeepers are responsible for cleaning and reporting any safety hazards to the homeowner or manager in charge. They must complete tasks like vacuuming, sweeping, emptying trash cans, dusting shelves, cleaning windows, and mopping floors. Some Housekeepers change linens, wash dishes, and do light ironing and laundry.

Houseowner

A houseowner is a person who owns the house or apartment that they live in.

Information about some jobs

Chef or Cook is responsible for using their culinary expertise to create appetizing dishes for diners to enjoy.

Gardener responsibilities include monitoring the health of all plants and watering,mowing lawns and keeping green spaces and walkways clear of debris and litter.

Maids are responsible for keeping residences clean and tidy. They perform various cleaning tasks, which includes vacuuming and sweeping floors and wiping down kitchen counters.
